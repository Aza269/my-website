Uzbekistan as a Regional Logistics Hub: Strategies,
Partnerships, and Infrastructure 2020–2030

With special focus on Uzbekistan-Korea Business Cooperation Strategy

Part of the Research Competition: Sustainable Development of Uzbekistan

Competition Details

CONFERENCE OVERVIEW

This high-level conference examines Uzbekistan's strategic position and development as a regional logistics hub connecting Central Asia with global markets. The event brings together key stakeholders to discuss infrastructure development, international partnerships, and policy frameworks that will shape Uzbekistan's logistics sector through 2030.

SPECIAL FOCUS

The conference features dedicated sessions on Uzbekistan-Korea business cooperation, highlighting successful joint ventures, knowledge transfer initiatives, and future investment opportunities in logistics and transportation infrastructure.

KEY TOPICS

  • Strategic positioning of Uzbekistan in regional and international transit corridors
  • Government initiatives and policy frameworks (2020–2030)
  • Infrastructure development projects and investment opportunities
  • Digital transformation in logistics and supply chain management
  • Uzbekistan-Korea partnership models and success stories
  • Sustainable development and environmental considerations in logistics
  • Regional integration and connectivity

PARTICIPANTS

  • Government officials and policymakers
  • Industry leaders and logistics specialists
  • Academic researchers and think tanks
  • International financial institutions
  • Korean business representatives and investors
  • Regional economic cooperation organizations
